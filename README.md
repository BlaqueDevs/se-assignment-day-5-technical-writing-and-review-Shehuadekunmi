## 1. How can understanding your audienceâ€™s expertise level (tech experts vs. regular folks) shape the way you present technical information?

1. Language and Terminology:

Tech Experts: Use industry-specific jargon and advanced concepts without over-explaining.

Regular Folks: Simplify the language, explain technical terms, and avoid overwhelming details.



2. Depth of Explanation:

Tech Experts: Dive deeper into technical details, provide in-depth insights, and explore complexities.

Regular Folks: Focus on the broader picture, explaining only the essential aspects and practical applications.



3. Examples and Analogies:

Tech Experts: Use industry-relevant examples and case studies.

Regular Folks: Rely more on everyday analogies that make complex ideas easier to grasp.



4. Visuals and Demonstrations:

Tech Experts: Incorporate technical diagrams, code snippets, and detailed workflows.

Regular Folks: Use simplified visuals, infographics, and step-by-step demonstrations.
